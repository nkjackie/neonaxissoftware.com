AI product stack

Designed as layers of infrastructure – each module can stand alone, or operate as part of a full stack.

Alpha Analytics

A data analytics layer focused on reliability and clarity. Ingests multi-source data, normalizes it, and surfaces actionable views for operations and strategy.

  • Unified event & metric pipelines
  • Signal-oriented dashboards
  • Enterprise-ready data controls

FlowSync AI

Workflow automation powered by AI, with guardrails. Connects APIs, data streams, and system events into auditable workflows.

  • Human-in-the-loop review points
  • Audit-friendly execution logs
  • API-first orchestration

QuantAI Engine

A modeling and backtesting layer for data-driven strategies. Built for research, simulation, and controlled live deployment.

  • Scenario & stress testing
  • Configurable risk constraints
  • Extensible factor library

Decision Oracle

Structured decision support that pairs AI models with transparent reasoning, helping teams evaluate scenarios and options.

  • Configurable decision templates
  • Traceable rationale output
  • Designed for internal tools embedding
